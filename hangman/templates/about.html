Hangman is a guessing game for two or more players. One player (Computer) thinks of a word and the other tries to guess it by suggesting letters or numbers, within a certain number of guesses.

Details: 
  • Max score
    Highest score among all the rounds of game. 

  • Lives
    Maximum remaining tries which can be incorrect 

  • Score
    Score for the current round of game
